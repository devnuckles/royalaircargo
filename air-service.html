Air Freight Service

Royal Air Cargo has provided air freight services widely and worked with almost all well-known major airlines. In these years of our business, we have built strong relationships and communications with key airline staff and have established excellent buying power ability. So, it becomes easy to pass that benefit on our clients.

Not only that, we have both block spaces in some major airlines as well as we also provide chartered programs. We try our best to transfer your all kinds of goods or/and products by following just in time shipping methods to be able to accommodate last-minute decisions in critical situations. We provide worthwhile solutions with prominence on space and price commitments.

Our freight forward services include:
  • Schedule services
  • Collection and forward of shipments from airport to airport
  • Handling critical goods by Professional personnel
  • Charted programs
  • Option of carriers based on your cargo needs
  • Customs clearance and documentation
  • Consolidation
